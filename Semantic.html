Facts about cats

If cats are wagging their tales, it shows annoyance but an upright straight tall which can have a curled end shows love as well as them licking you. Cats lick their hands and rub it across their heads to clean it.

Many different cat specides act differently when their owners leave them alone for a long time. Some are very attached and close to their owners so they get very sad while others are independent and show less emotion.
